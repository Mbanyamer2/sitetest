Intelligent Multimedia Management

Sparks Content Manager integrates in one single platform all the capabilities for acquisition, automatic classification, archive and distribution of multimedia content (videos, photos, audios and documents).


Our solution includes all the tools for a professional content edition (cuts, format changes, subtitles, etc.) allowing the content leader to combine in one platform all the utilities needed to answer the requests of all the departments (brand & communication, training or sales, for example). Also, each user can organize and customize the content according to the structure and operation that better suit its needs.



Among its differential aspects, the platform stands out for the use of artificial intelligence modules for the automatic analysis and classification of stored content, including facial and voice biometrics, object recognition in a scene and speech-to-text transcription and translation in multiples languages, among others. All this allows to locate immediately persons, objects or words.


Social Media and Multimedia Monitoring

Sparks Intelligent Media Monitor is a powerful software platform which offers a comprehensive solution for social media and mass multimedia monitoring. It integrates in a single platform all the necessary tasks for an effective media monitoring: captures all social media, TV and radio broadcasts as well as online platforms content, and subsequently applies artificial intelligence and advanced analyzers to automatically classify all the data



Our automated and intelligent Content Management and Media Monitor provides significant advantages for users: better quality and depth of analysis, and the optimization of necessary resources, especially where contents are still manually reviewed. Sparks Intelligent Media Monitor is an ideal solution for media monitoring agencies, corporate entities and government agencies.
